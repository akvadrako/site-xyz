I grew up reading the Arabian Nights and the stories have shaped my fantasy world.

For this headboard, I mostly thought of a story that doesn't exist in the original Nights, but it's with the same characters. \
Inspired by the great illustrators of the Nights: Virginia Frances Sterrett, Edmund Dulac, Léon Carré, Kay Nielsen.
